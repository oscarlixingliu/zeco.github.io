Welcome to Zeco!

Zeco is a non-profit organisation that is dedicated to reduce waste and promote environmental consciousness through biodegradable stationary. We use Computer Aided Design and Manufacturing technology to design and 3D print biodegradable stationary.

Biodegradability

The Z-pen is unlike an ordinary pen; it is a remarkable innovation that combines functionality with sustainability. Unlike traditional plastic pens that contribute to environmental pollution, the Z-pen is commercially biodegradable. The entire pen case is made of Polylactic Acid (PLA), a bio-based polymer derived from renewable resources such as corn starch or sugarcane.
The utilization of PLA in the Z-pen makes it an eco-friendly alternative to conventional plastic pens. When disposed of properly, the pen undergoes a natural degradation process, breaking down into biodegradable molecules. This decomposition ensures that the pen does not leave behind harmful residues or contribute to long-lasting waste accumulation.
