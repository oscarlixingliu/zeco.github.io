Meet our team members

Chief of Design: Ryan Shen

Ryan has a passion for art that allows him to generate ideas from a unique lens, and he consistently strives to enhance the design of our products. His familiarity with digital design tools such as Fusion 360 and Blender enables him to create intricate and visually stunning models that are also functional and cost efficient. With his own 3D printer, Ryan can swiftly transform his ideas into prototypes for testing and validation. Ryan's boundless creativity inspires the team to explore new horizons and push the boundaries of innovation. We are fortunate to have his skills and passion driving our product development forward.

With his tall stature and intelligent appearance, Oscar captivates attention and commands respect. He specialices in business management and coordination with the members of the business, enabling our operations to progress smoothly and efficiently. With Oscar's leadership, we are confident in achieving our goals as a business and making a positive difference in the world.
