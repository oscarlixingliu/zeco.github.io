Why choose Zeco?

Zeco's appeal

The Z-pen's angled body is visually appealing and designed for functional comfort, leaning onto your hand while you write. Its slim shape adds to its attractiveness. With an innovative and ergonomic design, it accommodates various hand sizes and grips, ensuring a personalized and pleasant writing experience. The pen's adjustable holds cater to different needs, providing comfort and ease. Additionally, the Z-pen offers customization options in colors, textures, and designs, allowing you to reflect your personality and style. Made of lightweight yet durable polylactic acid (PLA), it withstands accidental drops and lasts longer. Choose the Z-pen for a superior combination of style, practicality, and durability, and elevate your writing experience.

Our process

3D Printing

We use an in-house 3D printer to print out prototypes that we can test. This allows us to identify issues swiftly and to constantly refine our product when inspiration strikes.

Design: Fusion 360

We implement computer aided design(CAD) to create a 3D model of the product before it is printed. Software such as fusion 360 and Blender are powerful tools that help us turn imagination into reality.

Planning & Drawings

Our chief of Design, Ryan Shen, has utilized his extensive art skills and rich imagination to come up with the original design of the Z-pen. He continutes to make improvements through his sketches.

Collaboration

We make use of every member's creativity through discussions that enable us to come up with different improvements to the product and inspire each other.
